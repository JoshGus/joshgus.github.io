Image Breakdown

The drawing engine from my homepage, opened up. Drop in a photo and watch it get taken apart: Sobel gradients find the contours, k-means + connected components carve it into colour blocks, and a border-contrast saliency pass picks out the subject. Then pen particles redraw it — laying a base, then going back over the subject in finer detail. All of it runs in your browser.

The redraw

waiting for an image
1 · Colour blocking — broad fills sweep each segmented region, painting it in that block's own colour until the frame is covered.
2 · Linework — ink tracers follow the edge tangents and hatch ticks shade by darkness, drawing over the blocks.
3 · Refine — the fills retire and every particle re-aims at the detected subject, drawing finer still: thinner ink on subject edges and tighter hatching. Nothing thick runs again, so detail is never painted back over.
4 · Settle — particles are culled away and the loop stops, leaving the finished painting.
