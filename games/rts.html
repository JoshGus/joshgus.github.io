Frontline — How to play

v0.22.1
Objective
Destroy every rival team's base. Set up the match in the start menu: 2–6 commanders, teams, terrain, map size, and per-bot difficulty — or watch an all-bot match as a spectator.
Teams & allies
Commanders on the same team are allies — no friendly fire, and you can't command their troops. Open 📡 Comms to suggest attack / defend / gather / rally spots to allied bots; they weigh your ping against their own read of the field and answer in the AI log. Click a commander chip (or its row in the left panel) to inspect anyone's gold, research, queues, army and live brain state.
Economy
Spawn Engineers (base or Engineer Hall) — they auto-gather gold from deposits (⛏) and return it to your base or a Mine. Right-click a deposit to dedicate a miner, or a Mine to set a drop-off. Mines take per-mine profit upgrades.
Production buildings
Barracks trains infantry (Rifleman→Sniper/Rocketeer with Adv. Training). Vehicle Depot builds ATVs, APCs and Tanks (Heavy Division). Airbase fields Helicopters and Gunships (Flight Tech). Engineer Hall trains engineers and researches engineer upgrades.
Towers
Five tower types, each built by engineers: Guard (balanced), MG Nest (fast, short), Flak (anti-air only), Artillery (long-range AoE, arcs over walls) and Sniper Post (extreme range, needs line of sight).
Selecting & moving
Left-click / drag to select. Right-click ground to move, or right-click an enemy to lock on. Ctrl/Shift+click a portrait selects all of that type. Press . to grab idle engineers. Space pauses, +/- game speed.
Touch controls
On a phone or tablet: tap your unit or base to select, tap ground or an enemy while something is selected to give the order (the right-click), and drag across the map to box-select — a short drag over empty ground clears the selection. Two fingers drag to pan and pinch to zoom. The left panel tucks away behind the ▶ tab.
Camera & overlays
Scroll zooms to the cursor, middle-drag or WASD pans. Toggles: Ally/Heat density maps, Threat (enemy firing ranges), and 🧠 Overlay — see each bot's stance, objective, approach weights and team chatter.
Dev console
Press ` for the console. help lists commands — gold, spawn, speed, tech, reveal AI state, and more.
Engineers
Select engineers for ↩ Return Gold (bank what they carry) and ⛏ Mine Zone — click to mark areas they should mine (multiple; keeps them out of the firefight). Right-click any friendly building to repair it (cheaper with Salvage Kits). Medics take Seek / Hold / Heal-Zone orders.
Troop cap & standings
A troop cap (set at boot, raised by Field HQ / War Command) keeps armies from ballooning. The 📈 Graph tracks every commander over time — toggle By Team to sum allies. Use the right-side zoom slider for fine control.
Mortars & cover
Entrench Mortars for +60% range/damage (immobile). Engineers build Cover that soaks 50% of crossing fire (air ignores it) and a Hospital that heals nearby troops. Medics heal on the move.
Base defense
Your base auto-fires at nearby enemies. Buy base upgrades (range, cannons, reinforced walls), repair buildings, or call a one-shot Airstrike.
