Frontline — How to play

v0.13.0
Objective
Destroy the enemy base (right side) before they destroy yours. Build an economy, train an army, and push across the field.
Economy
Spawn Engineers — they auto-gather gold from deposits (⛏) and return it to your base or a Mine. Right-click a deposit to assign a dedicated miner, or a Mine to set a drop-off.
Selecting & moving
Left-click / drag to select. Right-click ground to move, or right-click an enemy to lock on — your units chase and stay in range until it dies, then hold. Ctrl/Shift+click a portrait selects all of that type. Press . to grab idle engineers.
Camera
The field is large — scroll to zoom to the cursor, middle-drag or WASD/arrows to pan. Top-bar toggles: Ally (blue friendly density), Heat (red enemy density) and Threat (overlapping enemy firing ranges — where you're targetable).
Building
Select an engineer, pick a structure, then click a spot. B cycles build types. Build Mines (economy, per-mine profit boosts), Barracks, Research, Guard Towers (upgrade to MG/Artillery/Sniper — needs an engineer + time), Hospital (heals nearby troops) and Cover (soaks 50% of crossing enemy fire; air ignores it — toggle targetable or have an engineer deconstruct it). Buildings block movement, so units path around them. Right-click a damaged building with an engineer to repair it for gold.
Your base
Click your ▣ Base (or the top-bar button) to open it — train units, buy base-defense upgrades, repair all buildings, or call an airstrike. Hotkeys still work: URifleman IHMG JShotgun OATV PGrenadier YSniper MMortar TTank HHeli EEngineer. Snipers/Mortars/Tanks/Helis need Barracks upgrades.
Mortars
Select a Mortar and Entrench it for +60% range & damage (can't move while entrenched). Set a target area to bombard blindly. Research Mortar Targeting to reduce scatter.
Terrain
Rock walls block movement and line of sight — route around them. Water can't be crossed but doesn't block sight, so units can fire across it. The battlefield is a dense jungle biome.
Base defense
Your base auto-fires at nearby enemies. Upgrade its range & cannons, repair buildings, and — at the top tier — call in a one-shot Airstrike anywhere on your side.
