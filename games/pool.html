How to play

Aim · Power · Spin · Shoot
Aim — drag anywhere on the table to rotate your aim (fine adjustments, no snap).
Power — drag the power bar.
Spin — drag the dot on the white ball for follow / draw / english.
Shoot — fire the shot. Cancel resets power without shooting.
Arrow keys nudge the aim; Space shoots; R rewatches.
