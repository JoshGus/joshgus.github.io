Privacy

What this site knows

A personal site. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no cookies.

Short version: I don't track you. There is no analytics script, no advertising and no third-party tracker anywhere on this site, and nothing sets a cookie. The only personal thing you can hand over is a username you choose for the multiplayer games — and you only need one to list a game publicly.

What is stored in your browser

These live in localStorage on your own device. They are never sent anywhere except where noted, and clearing your browser data removes them.

What the game server stores

Multiplayer and the daily leaderboards run through a small server I operate on Cloudflare. It holds only this:

  • Usernames — the name you chose, plus a one-way hash of your device token so nobody else can claim it.
  • Open game listings — game, join code, host username and player count, while a game is open. These delete themselves within a couple of minutes of the host leaving.
  • Daily scores — game, date, username and score.

No IP addresses are recorded in any of it. That is deliberate and it is the reason multiplayer works the way it does: games are relayed through the server rather than connected player-to-player, so other players never learn your address. The server code reads no address either — the only request header it looks at is the WebSocket upgrade.

What the server does necessarily handle is the content of game messages, since it forwards them between players. Those are relayed, not stored. The connection is encrypted in transit (wss) but not end-to-end, so treat in-game messages as visible to me and to Cloudflare, and don't put anything sensitive in them.

Camera

Two games — Darts and the hand tracker — can use your webcam, and only after your browser asks and you agree. The video never leaves your device. Hand tracking runs entirely in your browser; no frames are uploaded, recorded or sent to me or anyone else.

Choices you have

  • Don't pick a username. Everything except listing a game publicly and posting a score works without one.
  • Play privately. Games are unlisted by default; a private game shares nothing with the directory.
  • Clear your data. Clearing site data in your browser removes everything in the table above.
  • Have a username or score removed. Email me and I will delete it.
