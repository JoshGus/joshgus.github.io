How points are placed
X axis = Red channel
Y axis = Green channel
Z axis = Blue channel
Position = average of each photo's 5 dominant colors mapped directly to RGB coordinates. Dot color = most dominant hue. Swatches at axis ends show the most extreme photo in that direction.
